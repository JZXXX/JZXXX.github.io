I am a researcher at [Beijing Institute for General Artificial Intelligence (BIGAI)](https://www.bigai.ai/).

I received my Ph.D. degree from the [School of Information Science and Technology](https://sist.shanghaitech.edu.cn/) at [ShanghaiTech University](https://www.shanghaitech.edu.cn/), advised by [Prof. Kewei Tu](https://faculty.sist.shanghaitech.edu.cn/faculty/tukw/).

My research interests include:
- natural language processing
- embodied AI
- information extraction
- reasoning and reinforcement learning for language agents
- value-based communication and cognitive-inspired AI

This website contains my latest publications and research news.  
You can also find my profile on [DBLP](https://dblp.org/pid/257/1724.html).
