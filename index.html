Zixia Jia

I am a researcher at Beijing Institute for General Artificial Intelligence (BIGAI).

I received my Ph.D. degree from the School of Information Science and Technology at ShanghaiTech University, advised by Prof. Kewei Tu.

My research interests include:

  • natural language processing
  • embodied AI
  • information extraction
  • reasoning and reinforcement learning for language agents
  • value-based communication and cognitive-inspired AI

This website contains my latest publications and research news.
You can also find my profile on DBLP.
